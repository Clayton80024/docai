## NONIMMIGRANT INTENT AND ABSENCE OF PRECONCEIVED PURPOSE

Pursuant to {{intent_cfr_reference}}, the Applicant must demonstrate bona
fide nonimmigrant intent.

The Applicant's decision to pursue academic study arose organically after
entry into the United States and was not predetermined at the time of
admission.

The Applicant has continuously maintained lawful nonimmigrant status and
intends to depart the United States upon completion of the authorized
program.
